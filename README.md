***Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)***
**An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Adidas sales performance across regions, products, and retailers—providing actionable insights into revenue trends, profitability, and market distribution**



***1. Project Overview:***

Understanding sales performance is critical for driving business growth and optimizing strategy. This project focuses on analyzing Adidas sales data to uncover:

- Revenue trends over time
- Regional sales distribution
- Top-performing products and retailers
- Profitability and pricing insights

The dashboard enables "data-driven decision-making" for sales, marketing, and business strategy teams.



***2. Objectives:***

* Track overall sales, profit, and units sold
* Analyze monthly sales trends
* Identify top-performing regions, states, and retailers
* Evaluate product category performance
* Understand pricing and profit margins



***3. Dashboard Highlights:***

**Key Metrics**

-Total Sales: $900M
-Operating Profit: $332M
- Units Sold: 2M
- Operating Margin: 42%
- Price per Unit: $45


**Key Visuals**

*Total Sales by Month (Line Chart)*
 - Displays monthly revenue trends and seasonality patterns

*Total Sales by State (Map Visualization)*
 - Shows geographical distribution of sales across the U.S.

*Sales by Region (Donut Chart)*
 - Compares performance across regions (West, Northeast, Southeast, South, Midwest)

*Sales by Product (Bar Chart)*
 - Highlights top-performing product categories like men’s and women’s apparel

*Sales by Retailers (Bar Chart)*
 - Identifies key contributors such as West Gear, Foot Locker, and Sports Direct

*Filters (Slicers)*

  - Region filter
  - Invoice date range: Enables dynamic and interactive analysis


**Key Insights**

 - West Region Leads Sales Performance
 - Strong Sales Concentration in Key States
 - Top Retailers Drive Majority Revenue
 - Men’s Street Footwear is Highest Selling Category
 - High Operating Margin (42%) Indicates Strong Profitability
 - Seasonal Trends Visible in Monthly Sales



***4. Tech Stack:***

 - Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development and visualization
 - Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
 - DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI calculations and dynamic measures
 - Data Modeling** – Relationships between sales, product, and regional data



***5. Dataset:***

- Source: Adidas Sales Dataset (sample retail sales data)
- Features Include:
  - Sales transactions (date, revenue, units sold)
  - Product categories
  - Retailers
  - Regional and state-level data
  - Pricing and profit metrics
